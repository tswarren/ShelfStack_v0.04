## Cross-cutting rules enforced

- **Draft PO vs committed inbound:** Draft PO creation records demand context in audit details, but active demand supply state does **not** treat draft PO lines as committed or demand-specific coverage. Active inbound allocation begins only when PO lines are eligible under `DemandAllocations::InboundAvailability` (not on draft create).
- **No raw IDs:** Normal staff flows use lookups, vendor cards, and inbound PO row actions.
- **Supply language:** `DemandLines::SupplySummary` staff states shared across detail, capture preview, and workbench (unallocated, on-hand, inbound, vendor backorder, fulfilled).
